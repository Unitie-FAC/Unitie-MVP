Our Work: Supermalt Case Study

The Challenge:

Supermalt, a malt beverage brand wanted to increase their social media presence within the student demographic.


Our Solution:

We partnered Supermalt with The Big Clash Game Show, the largest on-going event show within the Afro-Caribbean student community. Supermalt sponsored the final show of the year and audience members were encouraged take selfies and upload their images on social media with various hashtags involving Supermalt.
